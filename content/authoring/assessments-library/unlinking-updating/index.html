  • Unlinking and Updating Assessments from Library

Updating an Existing Assessment

  1. Click on the "Add Assessment Icon"

  2. Click on the assessment you want to edit in the "Edit assessments" portion of the dropdown menu

  3. Change whatever it is you want to change

  4. Click "Update In Library"

Unlinking an Assessment

  1. Click on the "Add Assessment Icon"

  2. Click on the assessment you want to disconnect in the "Edit assessments" portion of the dropdown menu

  3. Click "disconnect"

  4. Codio will display a message confirming that the assessment has been successfully disconnected from the appropriate library. You should also notice the link icon is no longer present next to the assessment in the "Edit assessments" portion of the dropdown menu
