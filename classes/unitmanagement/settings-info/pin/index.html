  • Pinning a unit

You can pin a unit for your class. The unit will appear pinned to the top of the student dashboard when students log-in in order make it easy to find. Jump to the student dashboard to find out more about what students see.

To pin a unit.

  1. From the Classes area, click on your class to open it.

  2. Click on the Units tab, centre left of your screen.

  3. You will see the units you have assigned to your class. To pin a unit, click the blue triple dot icon to the right of the unit name, and choose Pin Unit

  4. The unit will highlight and a star will be shown to show it has been pinned.
