You can monitor student progress and time spent on individual units.

  1. From the Classes screen, click on your class to open it.

  2. Click on the Students tab, top centre of your screen.

  3. You will see all students in your class displayed on this screen. You will see progress data for units started and units completed alongside their names.

  4. To see an individual student's progress in more detail click on their name. You will then see an individual breakdown of auto-graded assessment results, along with time spent on each unit.
  5. To see a more detailed breakdown of each unit the student has attempted, click on the unit name. You will be able to view a breakdown of the challenges attempted along with corresponding results.
