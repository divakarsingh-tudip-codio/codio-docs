As the creator of your educational organization in Codio, you have administrator rights. This means that you can add and remove teachers from your organization, edit your organization information, manage your students dashboard settings, create grading templates and delete your organization.

To access the administration tools:

  1. Click on your profile picture (bottom left of Classes)
  2. Click the name of your school (under My Organizations)
  3. You will see admin options listed across the top of the page.
